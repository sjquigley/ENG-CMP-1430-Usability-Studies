Usability Studies are Fundamental to ALL Product Design

That's right. Any user product, from durable goods to services and even their packaging, go through some kind of usability testing. In Usabilty Testing ENGCMP1430 we'll learn the basics of UX research by first thinking about user personas and scenarios, then practicing methods for conducting usability studies, and finally thinking about how we might best communicate our findings and data to our clients. Along the way, we'll be learning and applying theories of information design and human factors to inform our thinking.
